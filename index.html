How This Works

Play a quick game to decide who gets to pick the movie and who picks the food!

  • Click “Pick a Movie” or “Order Food” to begin.
  • You’ll be prompted to start a Word Hunt game on iMessage.
  • Whoever wins gets 3 spins on a randomizer wheel to make the final choice.
  • Once decided, the countdown continues until movie night!
